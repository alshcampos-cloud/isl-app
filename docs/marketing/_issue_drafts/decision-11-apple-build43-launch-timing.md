## Context

Apple Build 43 didn't get approved in time for the April 22 launch attempt. Status today: in App Review queue. Once approved, the question is how to time the public launch — coordinated launch event or silent slip into App Store transition.

## Options

- **A) Coordinated launch beat** — LinkedIn essay (D3) + App Store live + Practice Prompter rebrand in-product all hit the same day. Maximum signal. Requires all three ready simultaneously.
- **B) Silent App Store launch first, marketing beats over following 2-4 weeks** — Lower risk of "launched and crashed" public moment. Time to fix iOS-specific bugs surfaced by real users.
- **C) Press-release / Product Hunt launch tied to App Store approval** — Higher reach but burns the "one good shot" at PH. Requires funnel + pricing + pixels all production-ready.
- **D) App Store live but unpromoted — focus marketing on web app** — Treat iOS as fulfillment channel, not marketing channel, until rest of funnel dialed.

## Recommended

**B** — silent App Store live, then coordinated marketing beats over 2 weeks. April 22 attempt slipped partly because we tried to coordinate too many beats at once. Smaller sequenced launches reduce concurrent risk (V.S.A.F.E.R.-M + HIGH-blast principles).
