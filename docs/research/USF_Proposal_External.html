The Gap

Your Nursing Students Are Interviewing Without Nursing-Specific Prep

General interview tools like Big Interview teach great fundamentals — STAR method, body language, confidence. But nursing interviews are different. Hiring managers ask about clinical judgment, SBAR communication, patient safety priorities, and care coordination. No general tool prepares students for that.

NurseInterviewPro fills the nursing-specific gap.

We don't replace Big Interview. We complement it. Your students keep their general prep AND get a dedicated nursing interview coach that speaks their language — SBAR, clinical judgment, prioritization, handoff communication.

Why Semester Access?

30-Day Pass

  • Focused window before interview season
  • Best for students with interviews already scheduled
  • Lower total cost
  • Can be timed around career fairs or hiring events

Semester Pass ✓

  • Students prep around clinicals, exams, and applications on their own schedule
  • Covers the full interview cycle — from early prep to post-rejection pivots
  • Students who don't land their first choice can keep practicing for the next opportunity
  • Better per-student value (deeper institutional discount)
  • Career Services can promote it once at semester start — no timing pressure
Nursing students don't interview on a fixed timeline.

A BSN senior might interview for ICU in October, get passed over, then try ED or med-surg in December. An MSN student might explore multiple specialties across the semester. Semester access gives them room to practice, learn from each experience, and keep improving — without losing access between interview cycles.
